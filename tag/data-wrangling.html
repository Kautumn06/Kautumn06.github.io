Once More, with Data Cleaning

Posted on October 17, 2018 in articles • Tagged with data-wrangling, IoT, capstone

Since over a year has passed since I finished my Capstone project, I decided that I wanted to try rebuilding the models to see if I could improve my original results. My team collected our own data using Raspberry Pi 3 and other devices to capture sensor data from our classroom. My primary role on the project was cleaning the data and building the models, classification models to predict the room's occupancy level and regression models to predict the number of occupants in the room. So to begin (again), I'm going back to the beginning, which means cleaning (and cleaning some more) messy IoT data.
